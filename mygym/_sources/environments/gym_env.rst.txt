.. _gym_env: 

GymEnv
======

The GymEnv is a prepared environment for training and testing various robotic tasks.
Visualized as a gym, it contains several specialized :ref:`workspace`, where the training can take place.
* baskets, collabtable, darts, drawer, football, fridge, maze, stairs, table, verticalmaze
